# NDPA / NDPC Readiness Notes

Status: Draft for Nigerian privacy counsel or DPCO review  

## Position

The platform processes personal and potentially sensitive identity data in Nigeria-facing workflows. Formal NDPA/NDPC obligations should be reviewed by Nigerian privacy counsel or a licensed Data Protection Compliance Organisation where required.

- phone numbers
- emails
- BVN/NIN-related verification data where enabled

- identity documents
- selfies/liveness evidence where enabled

- payment and wallet records
