## Platform Overview

Our platform is a comprehensive digital solution that transforms how insurance companies manage and monetize salvage assets. We've built an end-to-end system that handles everything from damage assessment to final sale through competitive auctions.

### What We Do

We help insurance companies:
- **Assess** damaged assets quickly using artificial intelligence
- **Auction** salvage items to verified buyers in real-time
- **Process** payments securely and automatically
- **Track** every transaction with complete transparency
- **Prevent** fraud through intelligent monitoring

## Operational Workflow

### Case Lifecycle

**1. Case Creation (Adjuster)**
- Document damaged asset with photos
- Record voice notes for context
- Capture GPS location
- Submit for AI assessment

**2. AI Assessment (Automated)**
- Analyze damage from photos
- Estimate repair costs
- Calculate salvage value
- Provide market data

**3. Manager Review**
- Review AI assessment
- Adjust valuations if needed
- Set reserve price
- Approve for auction

**4. Auction Process (Automated)**
- Notify verified vendors
- Conduct live bidding
- Extend timer for late bids
- Select winner automatically

**5. Payment & Documents (Automated)**
- Collect payment from winner
- Generate legal documents
- Facilitate digital signing
- Release funds to insurance company

**6. Pickup Confirmation**
- Vendor confirms pickup
- Admin verifies transfer
- Close case
- Archive records

- **Deposit Forfeiture:** Non-compliant winners forfeit deposits
- **Fallback System:** Automatic promotion of backup winners
