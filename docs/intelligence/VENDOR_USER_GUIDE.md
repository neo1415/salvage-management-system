## Price Predictions

### What Are Price Predictions?

Price predictions forecast the final winning bid for an auction based on:
- Historical auction data for similar items
- Current market conditions
- Bidding activity and competition levels
- Seasonal trends

**Benefits**:
- Avoid overbidding
- Identify good deals
- Make informed decisions
- Plan your budget

### Viewing Predictions

Predictions appear automatically on auction detail pages when you view an active auction.

**Prediction Card Shows**:
- **Predicted Price**: Expected final winning bid
- **Price Range**: Lower and upper bounds
- **Confidence Level**: High, Medium, or Low
- **Confidence Score**: 0-100 percentage

### Understanding Confidence Levels

#### High Confidence (75-100%)
- **Meaning**: Strong historical data, reliable prediction
- **Color**: Green
- **Action**: Trust the prediction, bid confidently

#### Medium Confidence (50-74%)
- **Meaning**: Moderate historical data, reasonable prediction
- **Color**: Yellow
- **Action**: Use as guidance, but be cautious

#### Low Confidence (0-49%)
- **Meaning**: Limited historical data, uncertain prediction
- **Color**: Orange
- **Action**: Use as rough estimate only

### Price Range Explained

The price range shows the likely final bid range:

**Example**:
- Predicted Price: ₦1,250,000
- Lower Bound: ₦1,150,000
- Upper Bound: ₦1,350,000

**Interpretation**:
- 50% chance final bid is around ₦1,250,000
- 80% chance final bid is between ₦1,150,000 and ₦1,350,000
- Wider range = more uncertainty

### How Predictions Are Calculated

Click "How is this calculated?" to see:

**Similar Auctions**:
- Number of similar past auctions analyzed
- How similar they are (make, model, year, condition)
- Their final prices

**Market Conditions**:
- Current competition level (low/medium/high)
- Price trend (rising/stable/falling)
- Seasonal factors

**Adjustments**:
- How market conditions affect the prediction
- Why confidence is high or low

### When Predictions Update

Predictions automatically update when:
- A significant bid is placed (>10% change)
- Market conditions change
- New similar auctions close

**Real-Time Updates**: You'll see a notification when a prediction changes while you're viewing an auction.

### No Prediction Available

Sometimes predictions aren't shown because:
- **New Item Type**: No similar historical auctions
- **Insufficient Data**: Too few past auctions to analyze
- **Unusual Item**: Unique characteristics make prediction unreliable

**What to Do**:
- Check the reserve price as a baseline
- Look at market value estimate
- Research similar items manually
- Bid cautiously

## Personalized Recommendations

### What Are Recommendations?

The "For You" feed shows auctions that match your interests based on:
- Your past bidding history
- Asset types you prefer
- Price ranges you typically bid in
- Damage levels you're comfortable with
- Auctions similar to ones you've won

**Benefits**:
- Discover relevant auctions faster
- Don't miss opportunities
- Save time browsing
- Increase your win rate

### Accessing Recommendations

1. Go to Auctions page
2. Click "For You" tab
3. See personalized recommendations ranked by relevance

**Refresh**: Recommendations update every 15 minutes with new auctions.

### Understanding Match Scores

Each recommendation shows a match score (0-100):

#### Excellent Match (80-100)
- **Meaning**: Highly relevant to your interests
- **Color**: Green
- **Action**: Review immediately, likely a good fit

#### Good Match (60-79)
- **Meaning**: Relevant, worth considering
- **Color**: Blue
- **Action**: Review when you have time

#### Fair Match (40-59)
- **Meaning**: Somewhat relevant, may be interesting
- **Color**: Gray
- **Action**: Browse if you're exploring new options

#### Low Match (<40)
- **Meaning**: Not typically shown (filtered out)
- **Action**: N/A

### Recommendation Reasons

Each recommendation shows why it was suggested:

**Common Reasons**:
- **Similar to Previous Bids**: You've bid on similar items before
- **Preferred Make/Model**: Matches your favorite brands
- **Price Range Match**: Within your typical bidding range
- **Damage Level Match**: Matches your preferred condition
- **High Demand**: Popular item you might be interested in
- **Closing Soon**: Auction ending soon, don't miss it
- **Low Competition**: Fewer bidders, better chance to win

### Improving Recommendations

Recommendations get better over time as you:
- Place more bids
- Win auctions
- View different asset types
- Interact with the platform

**Tips**:
- Bid on items you're interested in (even if you don't win)
- View auctions you like (helps the system learn)
- Use "Not Interested" button to refine recommendations

### Not Interested Button

Click "Not Interested" on recommendations you don't want:

**Effects**:
- Removes that auction from your feed
- Helps system learn your preferences
- Improves future recommendations

**Use When**:
- Asset type doesn't interest you
- Price is too high
- Damage is too severe
- Location is inconvenient

### Real-Time Notifications

Get notified when:
- **New Match**: A new auction matches your preferences
- **Closing Soon**: Recommended auction ending in 1 hour
- **Price Drop**: Current bid drops on recommended auction

**Settings**: Control notification frequency in your profile settings.

### For New Vendors

If you're new and haven't placed bids yet:

**Initial Recommendations Based On**:
- Asset categories you selected during registration
- Your account tier (tier 1 vs tier 2)
- Popular auctions (trending items)

**Getting Better Recommendations**:
1. Complete your profile with preferences
2. Browse and view auctions you like
3. Place your first few bids
4. System learns your preferences quickly (after 3-5 bids)

## Tips for Success

### Bidding Strategy

1. **Check Predictions First**: Always review price predictions before bidding
2. **Set Your Max Bid**: Decide your maximum based on prediction + your margin
3. **Consider Confidence**: Trust high-confidence predictions more
4. **Watch Competition**: Adjust strategy based on competition level
5. **Time Your Bids**: Use market insights to bid at optimal times

### Using Predictions Effectively

**Do**:
- Use predictions as guidance, not absolute truth
- Consider confidence level in your decision
- Factor in your own expertise and inspection
- Adjust for your specific needs and margins

**Don't**:
- Blindly follow predictions without inspection
- Ignore low confidence warnings
- Forget to account for additional costs (transport, repairs)
- Rely solely on predictions for major purchases

### Maximizing Recommendations

**Do**:
- Check "For You" feed daily
- Act quickly on high match scores
- Use "Not Interested" to refine
- Explore different asset types occasionally

**Don't**:
- Ignore closing soon notifications
- Dismiss recommendations without reviewing
- Stick only to familiar items (miss opportunities)
- Forget to update your preferences

### Market Intelligence

**Do**:
- Review market insights weekly
- Track trends in your focus areas
- Adjust strategy based on data
- Download reports for offline analysis

**Don't**:
- Ignore regional price differences
- Bid during peak competition times unnecessarily
- Overlook trending assets
- Forget to benchmark your performance

## Privacy and Data

### What Data Is Collected

The system collects:
- **Auction Views**: Which auctions you view
- **Bids Placed**: Your bidding history
- **Wins**: Auctions you've won
- **Session Data**: Time on site, pages viewed
- **Device Info**: Mobile vs desktop (for optimization)

**Purpose**: Improve predictions and recommendations for you.

### Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access Your Data**: Download all your intelligence data
- **Opt Out**: Disable personalized recommendations
- **Delete Data**: Request deletion of your data
- **Understand**: See how your data is used

### Accessing Your Data

1. Go to Profile Settings
2. Click "Privacy & Data"
3. Click "Download My Intelligence Data"
4. Receive JSON file with all your data

**Includes**:
- All predictions you've viewed
- All recommendations you've received
- All interactions recorded
- Algorithm settings used

### Opting Out

If you prefer not to receive personalized recommendations:

1. Go to Profile Settings
2. Click "Privacy & Data"
3. Toggle "Personalized Recommendations" off
4. Click "Save"

**Effect**:
- You'll see popularity-based recommendations instead
- Predictions still work normally
- Your data is no longer used for personalization
- You can opt back in anytime

### Data Security

Your data is:
- **Encrypted**: All data encrypted in transit and at rest
- **Anonymized**: Personal info removed from analytics
- **Protected**: Access restricted to authorized personnel only
- **Retained**: Interaction data kept for 2 years, then deleted

### GDPR Compliance

We comply with GDPR regulations:
- **Consent**: You consent by using the features
- **Access**: You can access your data anytime
- **Deletion**: You can request data deletion
- **Portability**: You can download your data
- **Transparency**: We explain how data is used

## Troubleshooting

### Prediction Not Showing

**Possible Reasons**:
- New or unique item type
- Insufficient historical data
- System temporarily unavailable

**What to Do**:
- Check back later (may appear as more data is collected)
- Use reserve price and market value as guidance
- Contact support if issue persists

### Recommendations Not Relevant

**Possible Reasons**:
- Limited bidding history
- Preferences changed recently
- System still learning

**What to Do**:
- Place more bids to help system learn
- Use "Not Interested" button
- Update your profile preferences
- Give it time (improves after 5-10 bids)

### Prediction Seems Wrong

**Possible Reasons**:
- Market conditions changed rapidly
- Unique item characteristics
- Low confidence prediction

**What to Do**:
- Check confidence level (low confidence = less reliable)
- Review similar auctions shown
- Trust your own expertise
- Report issue to support if consistently wrong

### Not Receiving Notifications

**Possible Reasons**:
- Notifications disabled in settings
- Browser notifications blocked
- Email notifications turned off

**What to Do**:
- Check notification settings in profile
- Enable browser notifications
- Check spam folder for emails
- Verify email address is correct

## Glossary

**Prediction**: Forecasted final winning bid for an auction  
**Confidence Score**: 0-100 value indicating prediction reliability  
**Match Score**: 0-100 value indicating recommendation relevance  
**Sell-Through Rate**: Percentage of auctions that resulted in sales  
**Competition Level**: Low, medium, or high bidding activity  
**Price Range**: Lower and upper bounds of predicted price  
**Market Conditions**: Current state of the marketplace (competition, trends)  
**Similar Auctions**: Past auctions with matching characteristics  
**Recommendation Reason**: Explanation for why an auction was recommended  
**Demand Score**: 0-100 value indicating market demand for an asset

## FAQ

**Q: Are predictions guaranteed to be accurate?**  
A: No, predictions are estimates based on historical data. Always use your own judgment and inspection.

**Q: How often do recommendations update?**  
A: Every 15 minutes with new auctions and changing market conditions.

**Q: Can I turn off predictions?**  
A: Predictions are always shown when available, but you can ignore them. Recommendations can be opted out.

**Q: Why do some auctions not have predictions?**  
A: Insufficient historical data or unique item characteristics make predictions unreliable.

**Q: How many bids before recommendations improve?**  
A: Recommendations improve significantly after 3-5 bids and continue to get better over time.

**Q: Can I see predictions for closed auctions?**  
A: Yes, you can see how accurate predictions were by viewing closed auction details.

**Q: Do predictions account for item condition?**  
A: Yes, predictions consider damage severity and specific damaged parts.

**Q: Can I export my bidding data?**  
A: Yes, download your intelligence data from Privacy & Data settings.

**Q: How do I report inaccurate predictions?**  
A: Email support@neminsurance.com with auction ID and details.

**Q: Are recommendations the same for all vendors?**  
A: No, recommendations are personalized based on your unique bidding history and preferences.
