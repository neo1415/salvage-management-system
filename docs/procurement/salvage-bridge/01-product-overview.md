# Product Overview

## What Salvage Bridge Provides

Salvage Bridge provides a white-label salvage recovery platform for insurers and salvage operators. The platform helps an insurer run its own branded salvage auction workflow for vendors, buyers, claims adjusters, salvage managers, finance officers, and administrators.

The platform supports:

- salvage case intake
- AI-assisted damage assessment support
- manager approval workflows
- auction creation and bidding
- verified vendor access
- wallet and deposit handling
- payment tracking
- document generation and signing
- pickup release workflows
- fraud alert review
- audit logs and reports
- configurable branding and business rules

## Who Uses the Platform

| Role | Typical Use |
| --- | --- |
| Vendors / Buyers | Register, verify identity, fund wallet, bid, sign documents, pay, and receive pickup instructions. |
| Claims Adjusters | Create salvage cases, upload evidence, record location/details, and submit cases for approval. |
| Salvage Managers | Review cases, approve/reject salvage, manage auctions, review KYC, and control recovery workflows. |
| Finance Officers | Monitor payments, reconciliation, wallet/deposit status, and settlement workflows. |
| System Admins | Configure branding, business policy, access, templates, users, and platform settings. |

## White-Label Model

Each insurer deployment is expected to operate with insurer-specific:

- database/environment configuration
- branding and public homepage templates
- business rules and auction policies
- document templates and legal details
- payment provider settings
- KYC provider settings
- email/SMS/push settings
- support contacts and domain configuration

Salvage Bridge remains the platform provider. The insurer-facing public experience should use the insurer's brand, while procurement and service-provider materials should identify Salvage Bridge as the vendor/operator behind the platform.

## Procurement Positioning

The platform can be presented as:

> A configurable salvage recovery and auction platform built for branded insurer deployments, with role-based workflows, verified vendor onboarding, wallet/deposit records, payment tracking, document signing, audit trails, and operational reporting.

Do not present the platform as formally SOC 2 certified, independently audited, or legally certified unless those external reports have been completed.
